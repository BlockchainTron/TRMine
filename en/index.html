How to start earning?
Install a TRON cryptocurrency wallet
To play from Computer, install the TronPay or TronLink extension
To play from Phone, install TronWallet iOS or TronWallet Android
Top up your TRX wallet in a convenient way for you
TRX mining process
First, you need to buy game coins at a fixed rate of 1 TRX = 25 Gold Coin
Then select a suitable enterprise and purchase it for gaming coins.
From now on, your business will start making a profit.
Profit distribution
Profits from your businesses are automatically calculated every hour.
All income received from your enterprises is equally divided into available funds and treasury funds.
Treasury funds can be spent on acquiring new enterprises to expand production and increase profits.
Free funds can be withdrawn at any time, they can also be spent on the development of your enterprises.
Referral system
Referral link is available to participants who have acquired at least one enterprise.
You will receive 10% of each replenishment of the balance by the person you have invited to your Free Money account.
Administration Commission
The administration commission is 10% of the amount of money spent on the construction of enterprises.
